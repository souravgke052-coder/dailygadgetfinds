## What to Look For (Buyers Guide)

### Capacity vs Portability

The 10000mAh power banks offer the best balance of capacity and portability. You can expect:
- 1.5 to 2 full charges for modern smartphones
- Enough for a full day without wall outlets

The 20000mAh options provide peace of mind but add significant weight. Consider whether you'll actually use that extra capacity.

### Fast Charging Matters

Modern phones support fast charging, but many budget power banks don't. Look for:
- **USB-C with Power Delivery (PD)**: Best compatibility
- **Quick Charge 3.0/4.0**: Qualcomm-based phones
- **22.5W/30W output**: Noticeably faster than standard 10W

### Port Configuration

Think about what you need to charge:
- USB-A: Legacy cables and older devices
- USB-C: Modern phones, tablets, and laptops
- Multiple ports: Charge phone + earbuds + smartwatch simultaneously

### Safety Certifications

Stick to known brands that include:
- Over-voltage protection
- Over-current protection
- Short-circuit protection
- Temperature management

## FAQ

**How many times can a 10000mAh power bank charge my phone?**
Most modern smartphones have 4000-5000mAh batteries, so a 10000mAh power bank delivers roughly 1.5-2 full charges.

**Is fast charging safe for my phone battery?**
Yes, modern smartphones have built-in protection. Fast charging only draws maximum power when the battery can accept it.

**Should I get a 20000mAh power bank for flights?**
Most airlines limit lithium batteries to 100Wh (roughly 27000mAh). The 20000mAh options are generally allowed in carry-on luggage, but always check airline-specific rules.

**Do I need a USB-C power bank?**
If you have a phone released after 2020, it almost certainly uses USB-C. USB-C also supports higher power output for faster charging.
