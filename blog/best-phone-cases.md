## Who Should Buy What?

### Get a Slim Case If:
- You prefer the original phone feel and aesthetics
- You upgrade your phone every 1-2 years
- You use a PopSocket or ring holder for grip
- You're stylish and case should complement your phone

### Get a Rugged Case If:
- You work in construction or active environments
- You drop your phone frequently
- You use your phone without much care
- You have kids or pets around

### Get a Clear Case If:
- You paid extra for a nice phone color
- You want basic protection with minimal bulk
- You're transitioning from a naked phone
- You want to see the MagSafe ring clearly

### Get a Premium Case If:
- You want the best materials and finish
- You appreciate craftsmanship
- You keep phones for 3+ years
- MagSafe integration is important

## What to Look For (Buyers Guide)

### Materials

**TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane):**
- Flexible and shock-absorbing
- Good grip
- Can be clear or colored
- Moderate protection

**Polycarbonate (PC):**
- Hard and rigid
- Good scratch resistance
- Often used for back plates
- Moderate protection

**Leather:**
- Premium feel and look
- Develops patina over time
- Good grip when new
- Standard protection only

**Aramid Fiber (Carbon Fiber):**
- Extremely strong and light
- Premium appearance
- Expensive
- Basic protection (thin)

### Drop Protection

Look for these features:
- **MIL-STD 810G**: Military-grade drop testing
- **Air cushion technology**: Spigen's trademarked corner protection
- **Raised edges**: Screen and camera protection
- **Reinforced corners**: First impact point protection

### MagSafe Considerations

If you use MagSafe accessories:
- **Official cases**: Best alignment, 15W charging
- **Third-party with magnets**: Good alignment, verify charging speed
- **MagSafe compatible**: May have weak magnetic hold
- **Ring only**: Adds some magnetic support without full magnets

### Grip

The biggest factor in preventing drops:
- **Matte textures**: Best grip, may attract lint
- **Leather**: Good grip, wears over time
- **Clear cases**: Poor grip, often slippery
- **Patterns**: Ribbed or geometric patterns add grip

### Yellowing

Clear case inevitability:
- **UV-resistant coating**: Extends clarity
- **Premium materials**: Yellow slower
- **Budget options**: May yellow in 6-12 months
- **Replacement**: Clear cases may need annual replacement

## FAQ

**Do all cases fit all phone models?**
No. Cases are model-specific. iPhone 15 Pro cases won't fit iPhone 15 or iPhone 14 Pro. Always verify the case listing matches your exact model.

**What's MIL-STD 810G drop protection?**
It's a military testing standard for environmental durability. It doesn't mean the case is military-grade in all aspects—just that it survived specific drop testing conditions.

**How often should I replace clear cases?**
Most clear cases begin yellowing within 12-18 months. If clarity matters to you, plan on annual replacement.

**Should I remove case for wireless charging?**
With MagSafe and standard Qi charging, you can keep most cases on. Thick metal cases or cases with magnet arrays may affect charging efficiency.

**Are wallet cases practical?**
They're convenient for minimalist carry but add bulk and require removing the case to access cards. Consider how often you actually use physical cards versus digital payment.
