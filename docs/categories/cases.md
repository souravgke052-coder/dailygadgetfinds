# Phone Cases & Protection

Your phone deserves protection that fits your lifestyle. A good case absorbs drops, resists scratches, and still lets you use your device comfortably every day.

## What to Look For

**Drop protection** — Military-grade (MIL-STD-810G) ratings indicate tested drop protection. Air pocket corners add shock absorption.

**Material quality** — TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) offers flexibility and grip. Hard polycarbonate provides rigid back protection. Hybrid designs combine both.

**Grip** — Textured surfaces and raised edges prevent slips. Matte finishes resist fingerprints better than glossy.

**Button covers** — Responsive, clicky buttons feel better than mushy ones. Test before buying or check reviews.

**Wireless charging compatibility** — Most slim cases work fine with MagSafe and Qi charging. Thick cases may require removal.

**Camera protection** — Raised camera cutouts prevent lens scratches on flat surfaces.

**Ports and speakers** — Precise cutouts ensure charging cables and audio jacks fit without struggle.

## FAQ

**Do slim cases protect enough for daily use?**
For most people, yes. Slim cases handle everyday drops and scratches. If you work outdoors or are clumsy, upgrade to a rugged case.

**What's the difference between TPU and polycarbonate?**
TPU is flexible, shock-absorbing, and grippy. Polycarbonate is rigid and scratch-resistant. Premium cases use both in layers.

**Do wallet cases interfere with wireless charging?**
Some do, especially with cards placed directly on the charging coil. Check for "wireless charging compatible" labels.

**How often should I replace my phone case?**
Replace when yellowed, cracked, or no longer snug. For clear cases, expect discoloration within 6-12 months of daily use.

**Are brand cases worth the premium?**
Official cases offer precise fit and guaranteed quality. Third-party cases at half the price often match or exceed protection.
